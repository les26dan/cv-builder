- **Method**: Pattern-based section extraction

**Current Processing Rules:**

## ⚠️ **IDENTIFIED LIMITATIONS IN CURRENT APPROACH**

### **Critical Quality Issues**
1. **Aggressive Truncation**: 70% of CV content lost (6800 → 2000 chars)
2. **Pattern Matching Failures**: Misses Vietnamese job titles, non-tech skills
3. **Context Loss**: Formatting and structure information discarded
4. **Section Fragmentation**: Experience bullets may be incomplete
5. **Skills Underrepresentation**: Tech-biased pattern matching

### **Real Impact on Kien Vu CV**
**What Gets Lost:**
- Detailed achievement metrics (3x improvement, 90% reduction, 200% growth)
- Complete bullet point descriptions
- Multiple work experiences beyond pattern matches
- Soft skills and Vietnamese language skills
- Education details and certifications
- Professional summary context

**What Gets Preserved:**
- Basic contact information
- Company names and job titles (if matching patterns)
- Date ranges (if formatted conventionally)
- Tech skills (if mentioned in English)
